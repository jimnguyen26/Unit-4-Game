CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a cystal you will add a specic amount of points to your local score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click in it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
